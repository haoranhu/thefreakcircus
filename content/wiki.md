## Wiki Hub

This page acts as the lore-support side of the walkthrough hub. Use it when you need context, not when you need branch optimization.

## Core Topics

- Pierrot: obsessive affection, melancholy, emotional volatility.
- Harlequin: trickster energy, provocation, dangerous playfulness.
- Jester: myth, narration, symbolic performance.
- Doctor: medical horror and fear as an experiment.
- Ticket Taker: mirrors, distortion, and reality pressure.

## Story Notes

The circus setting works because it mixes romance tension, dread, spectacle, and unreliable perception. Day 2 especially deepens the lore through Columbina, side-route encounters, and darker power structures.
