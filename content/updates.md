## Updates Hub

This page tracks broad update-related interest around The Freak Circus. It is the site-wide update timeline, not the dedicated page for Day 2 or Day 3 intent.

## What belongs on this page

- General development progress
- Official devlog references
- Broad status changes that affect how the site should organize guide content
- Pointers toward dedicated chapter or update pages when a topic becomes specific enough

## Why this page still matters

Even when specific topic pages exist, a broad updates page helps visitors understand whether interest is centered on new content, release-watch behavior, character additions, or shifts in how the game should be read and organized.

## What does not belong here

- Full Day 2 route context, which belongs on [Day 2](/day-2)
- Day 3 release-watch intent, which belongs on [Day 3](/day-3)
- Character-lore reading, which belongs in the [Wiki](/wiki) and character pages

## Use this page as a hub

Think of this page as the top layer of the update cluster. It should point visitors toward more specific pages once their intent becomes clear enough to separate into Day 2, Day 3, or lore-driven reading.
