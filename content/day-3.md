## Day 3 Prep Hub

If you're looking for a Day 3 walkthrough, this page is the preparation layer: saves, route expectations, and what to revisit before the next major story beat.

## What Players Expect From Day 3

- Higher route lock-in and stronger consequences from prior choices.
- More payoff between Pierrot and Harlequin.
- Further expansion of Columbina-related lore and side-character relevance.

## Save Advice

- Keep separate saves for Pierrot-focused, Harlequin-focused, and balanced routes.
- Duplicate before irreversible Day 2 choices.
- Label files by route intent and major branch flags.

If you're entering Day 3 from a focused romance run, cross-check your route assumptions with [Pierrot](/pierrot), [Harlequin](/harlequin), and the broader [Endings Guide](/endings).
