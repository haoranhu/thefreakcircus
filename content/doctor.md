## Overview

Doctor is one of the most unnerving figures introduced around the Cyan Tent material. His presence leans into medical horror, clinical detachment, and the idea of fear being studied rather than merely inflicted.

## Character Read

- He embodies controlled menace rather than loud chaos.
- His scenes emphasize experimentation, observation, and the reduction of emotion into something measurable.
- He expands the world by showing that the circus contains systems of control, not just performers.

## What To Watch For

Track how his dialogue handles fear, pain, and consent. Those details often matter more than surface-level threats in understanding his role.
