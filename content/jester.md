## Overview

Jester arrives as one of the most important Day 2 additions. He functions less like a simple side character and more like a narrator embedded inside the circus itself.

## Why He Matters

- He frames Columbina's tragedy and helps connect character mythology to present tension.
- His performances feel theatrical, but the information they reveal changes how players interpret Pierrot and Harlequin.
- He turns the circus into a place of memory and ritual, not just spectacle.

## Route Context

If you're using this page as part of route prep, revisit Day 2 scenes involving storytelling and symbolic reenactment. Jester scenes often matter more for interpretation than for direct romance progression.
