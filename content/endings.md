## Endings Guide

This all endings guide for The Freak Circus helps you organize saves, route branches, and replay paths so you can unlock every major outcome more efficiently.

## Before You Chase Endings

- Finish at least one natural run before optimizing every branch.
- Duplicate saves before tent pivots, trust pivots, and scenes that feel emotionally irreversible.
- Use labels that describe route intent, not just chapter progress.

## Strong Save Labels

- `pierrot-care`
- `harlequin-test`
- `balanced-lore`
- `doctor-branch`
- `mirror-pivot`

## What Usually Matters

- Whether you respond with comfort, curiosity, suspicion, or withdrawal.
- Which emotional bond you reinforce consistently.
- How much optional lore and side-character context you absorb before later route pressure.

If you're targeting a specific branch, compare your notes against [Pierrot](/pierrot), [Harlequin](/harlequin), and the broader [Routes Guide](/routes) before replaying from scratch.

## Fast Replay Strategy

1. Build three master saves.
2. Replay from the closest emotional pivot instead of restarting whole arcs.
3. Keep one lore-heavy save separate from romance-target saves.
4. Document what each slot is for the moment you create it.
