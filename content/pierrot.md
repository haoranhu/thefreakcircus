## Overview

Pierrot is one of the emotional centers of The Freak Circus. He is theatrical, unstable, and often deeply vulnerable beneath the painted performance.

## Character Shape

- Melancholic, obsessive, and emotionally charged.
- Strongly tied to route outcomes and player trust.
- Frequently positioned between tenderness and danger.

## Route Advice

If you want the clearest understanding of Pierrot, keep multiple saves and pay attention to moments where empathy, suspicion, or reassurance are being tested.
