## Overview

Harlequin pushes the story through contradiction, flirtation, and destabilization. She is playful on the surface but rarely safe to read at face value.

## Character Shape

- Mischievous and highly performative.
- Often functions as both rival and narrative catalyst.
- Her scenes sharpen the game's themes of manipulation and desire.

## Route Advice

Watch how you respond to her tests. Curiosity, resistance, and participation can all change how much of her subtext becomes visible.
