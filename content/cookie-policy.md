## Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how The Freak Circus Walkthrough Hub uses cookies and similar technologies.

## What this site does right now

This site is primarily a static guide website. At the time of writing, it does **not** run a full analytics or advertising stack by default.

However, it may still use:

- browser storage used for basic site behavior
- third-party embedded content
- external links that take you to official or third-party platforms

## Local storage on this site

Some parts of the site may store a small preference in your browser, such as remembering whether you allowed an embedded game experience to load.

This is used to improve functionality and does not exist to build a marketing profile about you.

## Embedded content and third-party technologies

When you choose to load or open embedded or externally hosted game content, the third-party service that delivers that content may use:

- cookies
- local storage
- session storage
- device or browser information

These technologies are controlled by the third party, not by this site.

Examples may include:

- official game hosting platforms
- external media or content delivery services

## Why these technologies may be used

They may be used to:

- load embedded content correctly
- remember session state
- apply security protections
- measure service performance
- support the third party's own product behavior

## Your choices

You can generally:

- decline to interact with embedded content
- clear cookies or browser storage in your browser settings
- block some third-party cookies in your browser
- use private browsing modes where appropriate

If you block certain technologies, some embedded features may not function properly.

## Changes in the future

If this site later adds analytics, advertising, personalization, or broader third-party tooling, this policy should be updated and a fuller cookie consent flow may become necessary.
