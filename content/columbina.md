## Columbina in The Freak Circus

Columbina is one of the most important lore-linked names in The Freak Circus, especially once Day 2 material starts pushing the story toward memory, performance, and darker circus history.

## Why Columbina matters

- She helps deepen the symbolic side of the circus rather than just the romance side.
- She is tied to how players interpret Jester-related material and theatrical reenactment.
- She gives extra weight to the idea that the circus is built out of old roles, repeated patterns, and unstable identities.
