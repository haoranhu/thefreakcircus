## Contact

If you need to reach the site operator about corrections, content concerns, or general site feedback, use the contact method listed here when available.

At the moment, this page does not include a live contact form backend.

If you plan to run outreach or feedback collection, a hosted form or email contact method can be added here later.

Typical reasons to get in touch include:

- reporting inaccurate route or update information
- flagging broken links or embed issues
- asking for corrections to character, ending, or lore summaries
- raising copyright or attribution concerns related to referenced material

If a direct contact method is added later, this page should be updated to reflect the active communication channel and expected response scope.
