## About This Site

This site is an independent guide and access hub focused on The Freak Circus. It is designed to help players find official access points, follow route-related topics, and understand major character and update references.

The project is aimed at players who want a cleaner path from first discovery to official access, route planning, and spoiler-aware character reading. Instead of acting like a mirror directory, it concentrates on helping visitors understand what to click, what to avoid, and how to prepare for multiple endings.

## What This Site Covers

- Official-access guidance
- Route and ending support
- Character notes and update tracking
- Spoiler-aware navigation for new players

## Editorial Approach

The site is written with an English-first structure and tries to separate beginner guidance from deeper route interpretation. That means early pages stay lighter on spoilers, while route and ending pages focus more on save planning, branching logic, and character-specific pressure points.

## Important Note

This is not the official game site and is not affiliated with the original creator or publisher.

For official release information, game status, and build availability, the creator's itch.io page and devlog should always be treated as the primary sources.
